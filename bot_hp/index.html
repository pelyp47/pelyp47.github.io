How to start?

To start working with the bot, you will need to:

  • Create a chat with your friends.
  • Add the bot to the group, as well as give it administrator rights.
  • Write ‘Letters distribution’ in the chat to enable receiving invitation letters from Hogwarts.

Character information:

  • ‘Notitia’ - general information about your character and their characteristics.
  • ‘Notitia Exponentus’ - a list of spells of your character.
  • ‘Notitia Pera’ - a list of items in your inventory.

Studying:

  • ‘Go to class’ - attend a regular class.
  • ‘Go to private session’ - attend an individual session.
  • ‘Go to the next year of study’ - move on to the next year of study.

Work:

  • ‘Go to work’ - get a list of available jobs and choose a suitable one.
  • ‘Finish work’ - get a reward for completing the work.

Store:

  • ‘Go to the store’ - go to the store.

Using items:

  • ‘Use heal potion’ - use a healing potion.

Dungeons:

  • ‘Current dungeon’ - view information about the current dungeon.
  • ‘Go to dungeon’ - get a list of available dungeons and choose a suitable one.
  • ‘Leave a dungeon’ - leave the dungeon lobby before starting.
  • ‘Start a dungeon’ - start a raid.

Duels:

  • ‘Duel’ (in response to a message from a potential opponent) - challenge to a duel.
  • ‘Accept a duel’ - accept a duel challenge.

*Further instructions on how to act during and after the duel will be given by the bot itself.
